Company Profile

We are International training organization that has being in the business of saving life and preventing untimely death through our uncompetitive resuscitation training. Due to sudden increase in the number of drop dead cases reported in Nigeria, Wetland Rescue Consult Academy in conjunction with Wetland Care Global came into an agreement to lunch a violent attack on this untimely death, through our international training centre that is highly loaded with internationally trained practitioners (doctors,Nurses, Paramedics, and medics) in collaboration with highly skill Nigerian medical personnel that are abroad trained.
